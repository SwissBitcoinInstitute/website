## Why is Bitcoin interesting?

Bitcoin is interesting for all the usual reasons: mythical creator, cockroach-like design, the worse-is-better ethos, being a libertarian lifeline, and so forth. But for me, it was also a new way of thinking. Once you see Bitcoin, you cannot unsee it - and the world is never the same again.
