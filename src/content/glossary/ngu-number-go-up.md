# NGU – Number Go Up

NGU is a meme shorthand for the expectation that the price of bitcoin will continually increase over time, which by itself attract new market participants. It references Bitcoin’s programmed scarcity and the long-term adoption trend, contrasting with inflationary fiat currency.
