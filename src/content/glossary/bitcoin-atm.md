# Bitcoin ATM

A Bitcoin ATM is a physical kiosk that allows users to buy or sell Bitcoin using cash or electronic payments. Depending on the machine, users can deposit fiat currency to receive Bitcoin or sell Bitcoin for fiat withdrawal. These ATMs often charge higher fees than online exchanges but provide local accessibility.
