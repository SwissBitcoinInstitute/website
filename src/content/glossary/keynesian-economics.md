# Keynesian Economics

Keynesian economics is a school of thought advocating active government intervention in the economy, particularly through fiscal and monetary policy. It contrasts with the Austrian School of Economics, which emphasizes market self-regulation. Bitcoin challenges Keynesian principles by operating outside state monetary control.
