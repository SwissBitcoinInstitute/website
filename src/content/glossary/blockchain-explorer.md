# Blockchain Explorer

A blockchain explorer is a tool for inspecting the contents of the blockchain. It allows users to search for blocks, transactions, and addresses, and to monitor confirmations and balances derived from the UTXO set. A populer blockchain explorer for Bitcoin is mempool.space.
