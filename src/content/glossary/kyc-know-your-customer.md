# KYC – Know Your Customer

KYC refers to regulatory procedures requiring financial institutions and exchanges to verify the identity of their clients. Common steps include collecting passports, addresses, and proof of funds. KYC undermines pseudonymity in Bitcoin and creates centralized databases that may be subject to leaks and surveillance.
