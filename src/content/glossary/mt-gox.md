# Mt. Gox

Mt. Gox was a Bitcoin exchange based in Japan that operated from 2010 until its collapse in 2014. It lost approximately 850,000 BTC due to mismanagement and theft. The event remains one of the largest exchange failures in Bitcoin’s history.
