# Custodial Wallet

A custodial wallet is a wallet in which a third party controls the user’s private keys. Exchanges and hosted wallet providers commonly offer custodial wallets. They provide convenience but reintroduce counterparty risk, violating the principle “not your keys, not your coins.”
