# Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP)

A Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP) is a formal document suggesting technical or informational changes to the Bitcoin protocol. BIPs provide a structured way for developers, researchers, and the community to propose, discuss, and document improvements before they are implemented. They cover a wide range of topics, including consensus rules, transaction formats, wallet standards, and best practices
