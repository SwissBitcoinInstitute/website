# Bitcoin Satoshi Vision (Bitcoin SV)

Bitcoin Satoshi Vision (Bitcoin SV) is a cryptocurrency that split from Bitcoin Cash in 2018 via a hard fork. It aims to implement what it claims is Satoshi Nakamoto’s “original vision” by enabling unbounded block sizes and on-chain scaling. Critics argue that such design compromises decentralization and security. Today Bitcoin SV it is much smaller in terms of adoption, network security, and economic activity compared to Bitcoin.
