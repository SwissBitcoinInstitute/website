# Satoshi (unit)

A satoshi is the smallest indivisible unit of Bitcoin, equal to 0.00000001 BTC. All balances and transactions on the protocol level are denominated in satoshis. The term honors Bitcoin’s creator, Satoshi Nakamoto.
