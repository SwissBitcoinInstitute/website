# CBDC - Central Bank Digital Currency

A central bank digital currency (CBDC) is a digital form of a country’s fiat currency issued and controlled by a central bank. Unlike Bitcoin, CBDCs are centralized, permissioned systems that allow issuing authorities to monitor, control, and potentially restrict transactions.
