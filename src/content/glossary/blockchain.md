# Blockchain

A blockchain is a sequential chain of  blocks, which are cryptographically linked by blocks hashes. It serves as a public, append-only ledger of all transactions. In Bitcoin, the blockchain is secured by Proof-of-Work, making historical records computationally infeasible to alter.
